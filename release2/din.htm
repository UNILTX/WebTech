The formation and development of Shanghai cuisine is not only related to the local people's living habits and rich resources, but also closely related to the local political, economic and cultural development.
Over the past hundred years, Shanghai cuisine has developed rapidly and gradually formed its own characteristics due to its location at the mouth of the Yangtze River among the sea rocks of the Mainland of China, mild climate, rich products, developed industry and commercial prosperity.

Shanghainese love eating salty-sweet food.For my family, we put sugar to each dishes.
For Shanghainese, breakfast and dinner are the two most important meals.
Breakfast and dinner also vary. In the past, some varieties were available only in the morning.
