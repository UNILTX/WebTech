Location Recommendation

When choosing a place to stay in Shanghai, look first at the traffic. Shanghai, which has nearly all subway lines, has 17 subway lines. And in these criss-crossing subway lines. Line 2 runs through hongqiao Railway Station and Pudong Airport.
I would recommend to live near any stop of line2, since almost every station has a shopping mall or scenic spot. For example, the LuJiaZui Station of line 2 will lead you to the Shanghai oriental pearl station. (picture on the left by photographer @GIOVANNA GRIFFO )


Accomodation Options

When stay in Shanghai, you can choose to live in hotel or in BNBs.You can find so many options according to your ideal price, travel plan and so on. If you want to experience to live in a old-style Shanghainese house, the BNBs will be the only option.
