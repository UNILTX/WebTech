Shanghai cuisine has many distinctive features:
First of all, pay attention to fresh materials. It selects seasonal food , mainly fish and shrimp from Jiangsu and Zhejiang provinces, and takes live fish as the best . There are live fish for guests to choose from all the year round, and it is cooked alive on the spot.
Second, there are many dishes with different seasons .
Third, pay attention to cooking methods and constantly improve.
The fourth is the change of taste preference . In the past, Shanghai cuisine was based on thick soup, thick sauce and thick taste. Later, it gradually changed to moderate marinades, with light and elegant sauces and thick oil and red sauce. It pays attention to freshness, tenderness, color and salty taste.
What's more, Shanghainese formal dinner has the order of food: cold dishes, hot dishes, dim sum, soup. And here, let's focus on the first three.

    • Entrees are not limited to braised dishes with thick oil and red sauce, but also stir-fried and steamed.

      In Shanghainese cuisine, the vegetables in the hot dishes are exquisitely-cooked, and a variety of stir-fried, wine-flavoured salads and soups are even more enticing than meat dishes.
